## Teaching Interests

Corporate Finance, Portfolio Management, Personal Finance, Investments

## Teaching Philosophy

My teaching emphasizes practical application, conceptual clarity, and the development of analytical decision-making skills. I aim to help students connect finance theory with real-world financial decisions, market behavior, and corporate practice. In the classroom, I use examples, cases, data, and applied exercises to make finance accessible and relevant while encouraging students to think critically and communicate their reasoning clearly.
