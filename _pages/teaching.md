## Teaching Philosophy

<div class="teaching-intro">
My teaching emphasizes practical application, conceptual clarity, and analytical decision-making. I aim to help students connect finance theory with real-world financial decisions, market behavior, and corporate practice. In the classroom, I use examples, cases, data, and applied exercises to make finance accessible and relevant while encouraging students to think critically and communicate their reasoning clearly.
</div>
